About Pune Institute of Computer Technology

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT), an elite academic Institute located in Pune (India), which is rightly known as “The Oxford of the East”, playing an inspiring role in the education sector since its establishment in 1983. PICT believes in value-based quality education in the field of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and we are constantly endeavouring to achieve higher levels of technical excellence.
